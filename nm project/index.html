📘 Educational Resources

Forward Difference:
\( f'(x_0) = \frac{1}{h}[\Delta y_0 - \frac{1}{2}\Delta^2 y_0 + \frac{1}{3}\Delta^3 y_0 - \cdots] \)

Backward Difference:
\( f'(x_n) = \frac{1}{h}[\nabla y_n + \frac{1}{2}\nabla^2 y_n + \frac{1}{3}\nabla^3 y_n + \cdots] \)

Stirling:
\( f'(x_0) = \frac{1}{h} \left[ \frac{\Delta y_0 + \nabla y_0}{2} - \frac{\Delta^3 y_0 + \nabla^3 y_0}{12} + \cdots \right] \)
